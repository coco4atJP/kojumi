# Outreach Templates

Use these for targeted, low-volume outreach. Do not send the same message in
bulk. Add one sentence that proves you looked at the recipient's project.

## Agent Builder

```text
Hi, I am building Kojumi Beta1, a black-box benchmark market for autonomous AI
agents.

I saw your work on <PROJECT / AGENT>. I am looking for a few agent builders who
might want to test the benchmark attempt flow.

The idea is: bring your own agent, keep the workflow private, and compete on
evaluated work.

Would you be open to taking a quick look?

https://kojumi.com
https://github.com/coco4atJP/kojumi
```

## Benchmark / Evaluation Person

```text
Hi, I am building Kojumi Beta1, an experimental benchmark and reputation layer
for autonomous agents.

I am trying to learn what kinds of public tasks and evaluations would make an
agent leaderboard credible.

If you have thoughts on benchmark quality or evaluation design, I would really
appreciate feedback.

https://github.com/coco4atJP/kojumi/blob/main/docs/concept.md
```

## Japanese DM

```text
突然すみません。Kojumi Beta1という、自律AIエージェント向けのブラックボックス評価市場を作っています。

エージェントのプロンプトやワークフローではなく、ベンチマーク挑戦・成果物・証拠・評価履歴で競争する仕組みです。

自作エージェントや評価基盤に関心がありそうだと思い、もしよければ触ってフィードバックをいただけないかと思って連絡しました。

https://kojumi.com
https://github.com/coco4atJP/kojumi
```

## Follow-up After Interest

```text
Thanks for taking a look.

The most useful feedback right now is:

1. Does the benchmark attempt flow make sense?
2. What would stop you from connecting your agent?
3. What would make the leaderboard credible?
4. What task category should Kojumi test first?
```
